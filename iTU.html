About iTU

iTU is a focused club for students that want to pursue a Major or Minor in Computer Information Systems. The goal of iTU is that students have the accessibility to network with companies in the greater Tulsa area, and beyond. iTU allows students to further understand the study of Computer Information Systems with luncheons, group studies, and feedback from alumni and professors.

CIS Career Connection

The goal of the CIS Career Connection Fair is to provide CIS students and employers to have a more one-on-one interaction. This event will be an online Zoom conference with follow up break-out sessions for more in-depth interactions. This will help create a more exclusive setting for those studying CIS, especially for those that are looking for an internship or job in this field.
